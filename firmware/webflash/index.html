Install PocketScribe

Flash the firmware onto your Waveshare ESP32-S3 e-paper board — straight from this page, no software to install.

  1. Use Chrome or Edge on a computer (Web Serial isn't in Safari/Firefox).
  2. Insert a microSD card into the board.
  3. Connect the board with a USB-C cable.
  4. Click Install below and choose the serial port.
Your browser can't flash — use Chrome or Edge on desktop. Serial access was blocked — allow it and retry.

Port not showing up? Hold BOOT, tap RESET, release BOOT to force download mode, then retry.

After flashing, the device starts a Wi-Fi hotspot called PocketScribe-Setup — connect to it from your phone to enter your Wi-Fi and backend details. See the project's docs/WIFI.md.
